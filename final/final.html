About the CIRC

This website is intended to help users find what bank will best fit their needs. Here you can use our calculator that will allow you to enter your banking needs, and get what banks will best suit you. This website will be used by people across the nation to find banks specifically catered to their needs.

Below is our "Bank Rating Calculator". It is for you (the user) to input your own percentage into each of our different catogories of services that each bank may or may try to provide for you the consumer. You will give each an individual score which all together will be out of 100% depending on the level of importance a catogory and/or service is to you. After yo are done you will click the button to calculate the scores and we will provide a list of banks that moslty likley fit you. They will ranked pertaining to the scores you provided along with the score we calculated from this. So go on and try!
